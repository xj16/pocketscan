How the parser thinks

01

Rank the total

Lines with total / toplam / amount due beat subtotal, tax, KDV, change. The largest surviving amount wins — never a tax line.

02

Read either money format

1,234.56 and 1.234,56 both parse to the same cents by treating the last separator as the decimal point.

03

Disambiguate the date

ISO wins outright; otherwise US MM/DD and EU/TR DD.MM are resolved by which reading forms a valid calendar date.

04

Detect the currency

Symbols and codes — $ € £ ₺, USD TRY TL — map to ISO-4217, Turkish keywords included.
